About

At Maunga Club, we're all about creating unforgettable mountain experiences for our esteemed Club members. Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of New Zealand, our exclusive chalets offer the perfect blend of comfort and adventure.

Luxury Winter Chalets

Our chalets are more than just accommodations; they're your home away from home on the mountain. Exclusively available to our valued Club members, these cozy cabins offer shared lodging options catering to groups ranging from 6 to 40 people per chalet.

Mountain Convenience

Located directly on the mountain slopes, our chalets provide unparalleled accessibility. Ski in and ski out from your doorstep, allowing you to maximize your time on the 550-hectare skiing and snowboarding wonderland. With the longest ski/snowboard season Mountain Convenience

Booking Information

To secure your mountain retreat, keep in mind that chalet bookings are available exclusively for Club members. All reservations span from Friday to Sunday, ensuring you have a full weekend to relish the mountain experience.
